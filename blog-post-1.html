Fundamentals of AI , its history and core principlesin extensible

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines. It encompasses various subfields like machine learning, neural networks, and deep learning.

AI’s history dates back to early computing with significant milestones such as Alan Turing's Turing Test. Today, AI applications are pervasive, impacting areas from healthcare diagnostics to financial modeling.

Understanding AI involves grasping concepts like algorithms, data processing, and model training. As AI evolves, ethical considerations around bias, transparency, and privacy become paramount. This post provides a foundational overview, equipping readers with essential knowledge to appreciate AI's role in modern technology.
